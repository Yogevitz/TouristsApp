Our site describes the most beautiful and romantic city in the world, that is Rome.
Rome is the capital of Italy. The city is spread across the two banks of the Tiber River in the Lazio region.

In the past it was the capital of the Roman Empire, which controlled large areas in Europe and around the Mediterranean Sea. After the collapse of the empire, Rome continued to serve as the center of the Roman Catholic religion, as well as a cultural, economic and especially world-class cultural center.

Rome itself has 2.7 million inhabitants. In the surrounding cities, another one million residents live. The city center is spread out between seven hills east of Tiber. On the other side of the river is the Vatican Hill, which also belongs to Rome. Around the crowded center lie suburban areas within an enormous radius of the city. Among the suburbs are agricultural fields and many open areas, some within a few kilometers of the center.
